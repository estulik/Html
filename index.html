Introduction

Sprachcafé Style Mahjong, also known as Cantonese or Hong Kong Mahjong, is a popular variation characterized by its fast pace and exciting bonus rules.

Basic Setup

Players: 4 players
Tiles: 136 tiles (no flowers or seasons)
Starting Wind: Determined by dice roll
Dealer: East Wind starts the game

Tile Combinations & Actions

Pong (碰)

What it is: 3 identical tiles

When to use: When any player discards a tile that matches 2 tiles already in your hand

How to declare: Say "Pong!" immediately and take the discarded tile

Kong (杠)

What it is: 4 identical tiles

Two Types:

  • Concealed Kong: All 4 tiles come from your hand. ALL other players must immediately pay 1 yuan bonus fee.
  • Exposed Kong: Using a discarded tile to complete 4 of a kind. ONLY the discarder pays 1 yuan bonus fee.

Chi (吃)

What it is: 3 consecutive numbers in the same suit

When to use: ONLY from the player immediately to your left

Winning (Hu 胡)

Standard Winning Hand: 4 sets + 1 pair
