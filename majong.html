Pong (Á¢∞)

What it is: 3 identical tiles

When to use: When any player discards a tile that matches 2 tiles already in your hand

How to declare: Say "Pong!" immediately and take the discarded tile

Placement: Place the 3 tiles face-up on your side and discard one tile to end your turn

Effect: Play continues from your position

Kong (Êù†)

What it is: 4 identical tiles

Two Types:

  • Concealed Kong: All 4 tiles come from your hand (declare after self-draw)
  • Exposed Kong: Using a discarded tile to complete 4 of a kind

How to declare: Say "Kong!" loudly and place tiles face-up

When a player declares Concealed Kong, ALL other players must immediately pay 1 yuan bonus fee to that player.

When a player declares Exposed Kong, ONLY the player how discarded the tile used for Kong must pay 1 yuan bonus fee to that player.

After Kong: Draw a supplement tile from the back end of the wall

  2. Break the wall: Dealer rolls dice to determine where to break the wall
  3. Draw starting tiles: Each player draws 13 tiles (in sets of 4-4-4-1)
  4. Dealer starts: Dealer draws 14th tile to begin the game
  5. Game proceeds: Players take turns drawing and discarding tiles
